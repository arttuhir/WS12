ABOUT

Our recording studio was established in February 29th 1968. We are situated in the serene woods of Canada guarded by overtly agressive moose. What started as a solitary esoteric synthesizer experiment in a humble cabin in the woods is now a world-class studio complex. We specialize in recording music in the analog domain, but we are not afraid of the digital realm. In fact, we're quite adept at it also.

Pictured: Control Room 1 & 2

Our clientele is quite prestigious and you have undoubtedly come across their music in one way or another. We have made history and so should you.

We strive for the best. The best begins with the right people and we have them in spades. Every single person who works here has a passion for capturing the best performances in the most musically rich way. We always keep the end-product in mind, but we are not afraid to wander off from the beaten path.

We are equipped with the best gear in pro-audio and also have many custom-built pieces. Our large collection of vintage and current synthesizers and drum machines is a treasure trove of inspiration. Our guitars and bases ooze the spirit of music and our drums ring triumphantly in our hallowed live rooms.
